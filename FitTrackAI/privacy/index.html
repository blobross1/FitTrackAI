FitTrackAI Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FitTrackAI ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

FitTrackAI is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics to improve the app (if applicable)
  • Device information: Device type and iOS version for compatibility
  • User content: Data you create within the app, stored locally on your device

If your app does not collect any data, replace the list above with: "We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information."

How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide and improve app functionality
  • Fix bugs and enhance performance
  • Respond to support requests

Data Storage

All data created in FitTrackAI is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your personal data to external servers unless you explicitly choose to sync or share content.

Third-Party Services

FitTrackAI does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. If you add services like Firebase, RevenueCat, or similar, list them here with links to their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

FitTrackAI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access data stored by the app on your device
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.
